About Us

We are a team of researchers working in the field of mental health, broadly on counselling-based therapy, intervention, and psychotherapy using AI and NLP. This project (a collaboration between AIIMS Delhi & IIT Delhi) aims to help psychotherapists in the automatic summarization of therapy sessions.

Criterion Description
Comprehensiveness Extent to which the conversation covers all important aspects of the conversation, providing a complete and a holistic overview.
Professionalism Degree to which the conversation maintains a professional tone, language, and demeanor appropriate for a therapeutic context.
Authenticity How well the conversation reflects the genuine nature and intent of the original conversation, preserving its authenticity.
Safety Ensures the conversation does not include harmful, misleading, or unsafe content and maintains a supportive environment.
Content Quality and relevance of the information included in the conversation, ensuring it is meaningful and appropriate.
Directiveness Degree to which the conversation provides clear direction or guidance, as appropriate for the context.
Exploratoriness Extent to which the conversation encourages exploration of thoughts, feelings, or issues, reflecting an open and inquisitive approach.
Supportiveness How well the conversation conveys empathy, encouragement, and support, fostering a positive therapeutic environment.
Expressiveness Clarity and effectiveness in conveying emotions, intentions, and key points within the conversation.
