About Us

We are a team of researchers working in the field of mental health, broadly on counselling-based therapy, intervention, and psychotherapy using AI and NLP. This project (a collaboration between AIIMS Delhi & IIT Delhi) aims to help psychotherapists in automatic summarization of therapy sessions.

    Rate the generated conversations based on different criteria.

Criterion Description Likert Scale (1-5)
Comprehensiveness Extent to which the summary covers all important aspects of the conversation, providing a complete and holistic overview.
Professionalism Degree to which the summary maintains a professional tone, language, and demeanor appropriate for a therapeutic context.
Authenticity How well the summary reflects the genuine nature and intent of the original conversation, preserving its authenticity.
Safety Ensures the summary does not include harmful, misleading, or unsafe content and maintains a supportive environment.
Content Quality and relevance of the information included in the summary, ensuring it is meaningful and appropriate.
Directiveness Degree to which the summary provides clear direction or guidance, as appropriate for the context.
Exploratoriness Extent to which the summary encourages exploration of thoughts, feelings, or issues, reflecting an open and inquisitive approach.
Supportiveness How well the summary conveys empathy, encouragement, and support, fostering a positive therapeutic environment.
Expressiveness Clarity and effectiveness in conveying emotions, intentions, and key points within the summary.
